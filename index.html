Cardano RealFi Consortium

We are projects with real world utility

We connect Cardano blockchain transactions and tokens to real world assets and processes. Many of our projects also include a DeFI and/or ESG impact component.

The Consortium was founded at the Cardano Summit in November 2022 which included a speaker panel session about Cardano RealFi.
