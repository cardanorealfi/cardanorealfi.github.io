About Us

Welcome!

We are an independent collective of RealFi projects that create value and trust by connecting real world assets and processes to transactions and tokens on the Cardano blockchain.

The CRFC is an open and permissionless organization that models and advances decentralization principles.

We hold bi-weekly planning meetings and have organised the following working groups:

  1. Identity & Trust
  2. Fiat Conversion
  3. Document Verification
  4. Governance & Membership
  5. Outreach & Marketing

Find out more in our Resources section.
